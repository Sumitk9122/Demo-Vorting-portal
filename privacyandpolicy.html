Privacy and Policy

At Voting Portal, we are committed to protecting your privacy and the confidentiality of your personal information. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and share the information we collect from you when you use our voting portal.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information from our users:

  • Personal Information: We collect personal information such as your name, email address, and location when you register to vote on our portal.
  • Voting Information: We collect your voting information, including your vote selections, when you cast your vote on our portal.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect from you to:

  • Verify your identity and eligibility to vote.
  • Process and count your vote.
  • Communicate with you about our voting portal and updates to our policies.
  • Analyze and improve our voting portal.

Sharing of Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your personal information in the following circumstances:

  • With trusted third-party service providers who help us operate our voting portal and process your vote.
  • When required by law or to protect our rights or the safety of others.
  • In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of our company or assets.

Your Choices

You can control the information you provide to us and how we use it in the following ways:

  • You can opt-out of receiving promotional emails from us by clicking the “unsubscribe” link at the bottom of our emails.
  • You can update or delete your personal information by contacting us at [contact email].

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure.

Changes to Our Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We encourage you to review our Privacy Policy periodically.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy, please contact us at [contact email].
